\documentclass[a5paper, 12pt]{article}
\usepackage{handwrittennote}

\begin{document}

\noteheader{May 1956}
\notegreeting{Dear Friends,}

I am writing this message for you in case I am captured before I manage to recover my treasures.
Terrible thieves have been following me for the last week.
I fear they are close to grabbing me! 

I've left my map in the smuggler's hut, close by.
Take the right-hand path and look out for the abandoned building in the dell. 

If I don’t manage to make it back there before I'm caught, please rescue the treasure and save it until I can meet up with you.

\notesignoff{All the best on your hunt!}{\signature}
\end{document}
