\noteheader{June 1926}
\notegreeting{Thank goodness! You found the hut. Now the adventure can truly begin.}

I had to hide my tools: they would have given away my schemes!
You'll need to retrieve those before you can get to the prize.

First, the compass! 
It's in a leather pouch, and has with it instructions for what to do next.
I hid it close to one of my favourite spots.
Here is a clue as to where that is.

\begin{riddle}
It has a mouth but never speaks,\\
A giant cone where hot wind shrieks,\\
It holds a fire deep down inside,\\
Where boiling stone and shadows hide,\\
It isn't a chimney,\\
It isn't a hill,\\
But when it wakes it won't be still.
\end{riddle}
